Ramkrishna Sarada Model School, also known as Sri RKM Sarada Vidyalaya Model School , is a well-known educational institution in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 1938 by Sister R.S. Subbulakshmi, the school was established with a mission to educate and uplift underprivileged children, especially girls. It operates under the guidance of the Ramakrishna Math and is dedicated to fostering values inspired by the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna and Sarada Devi. The school offers education affiliated with the Tamil Nadu State Board, providing classes from the primary level to higher secondary. It is located in T. Nagar, Chennai, and has modern facilities, including a library, computer and science labs, smart classrooms, and extracurricular programs like music, arts, and yoga. The school is known for supporting first-generation learners, offering additional coaching and scholarships to students from low-income families. For admissions, forms are available both online and offline, with interviews and basic requirements for documents like birth certificates and Aadhar cards. The school also offers a range of activities for holistic student development, from sports and arts to robotics and life skills programs.
